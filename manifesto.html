manifesto

Why we created Adok?

Because we don’t want meetings where nobody is listening anymore, where screens are a barrier to coproductivity. Because we live in a world surrounded by screens, which are made for one user only. Because we believe it is fundamentally a hardware matter.

Let’s stop the powerpoint ! Let’s stop unilateral presentations !

Users are demanding for sharing and interactivity.

We believe that technology should gather people, reunite them. We think that technology should be more inclusive, made for the moments we spend together.

With Adok, we gather everybody around the table again, around the content, for the moments of sharing. We improve exchanges between people with a new sharing environnement.

We created Adok so the future won’t be a Wall-E kind of vision, with everybody living behind a screen, or with a Virtual Reality Mask, away from the moment.

Adok, a new way to be together.
